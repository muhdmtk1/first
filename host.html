How to Cook Fried Rice

I am muhd muttaka i will show you how to make a delicious fried rise

Step 1: Prepare Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (day-old is best)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, corn)
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 green onion, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Step 2:

Heat oil in a large pan over medium heat.

Step 3:

Scramble the eggs until fully cooked. Remove and set aside.

Step 4:

Add mixed vegetables and stir-fry for 2 minutes.

Step 5:

Add the cooked rice and soy sauce, mixing well.

Step 6:

Add the scrambled eggs and green onions, and stir. Season with salt and pepper.

Step 7:

Serve hot and enjoy!
