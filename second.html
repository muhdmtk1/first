How to Cook Fried Rice

Easy step-by-step guide

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably cold)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, corn)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oil (vegetable or sesame)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large pan or wok over medium heat.
  2. Add garlic and onion, and sautÃ© until fragrant.
  3. Add the vegetables and stir-fry for 3-4 minutes.
  4. Push veggies to one side, add eggs, scramble until cooked.
  5. Add the cooked rice and mix everything together.
  6. Pour in soy sauce, stir well to combine.
  7. Season with salt and pepper, cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy your fried rice!
